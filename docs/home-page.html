Keeping the Oldies Young

What does it mean?

For decades, music of the generations before us have been termed "oldies". Today, some of the most legendary music has been lost, covered by the technology and autotune of today's artists. As Ziggy Marley said, "Old music used to mean something. There is none of that today".

Growing up surrounded by some of the most amazing music of my parents' time, it has strongly influenced how I look at music. For that reason, I want to share some of the songs that remain my favorites today, as well as some of the recreations modern artists have made of those very songs.
